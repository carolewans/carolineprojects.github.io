Chimonica Loan Application Process

ChiMonica Bank, a well-established financial institution, faces challenges in its loan department due to a manual and paper-heavy process. These inefficiencies have led to prolonged loan approval times, causing customer dissatisfaction and hindering the bank's ability to remain competitive. The reliance on outdated procedures has also increased the risk of errors and compliance issues. To address these concerns, the project will include a comprehensive Business Requirements Document (BRD) and a gap analysis to identify discrepancies between current processes and industry best practices. This analysis will provide a roadmap for automating and optimising the loan approval workflow, ultimately improving customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
